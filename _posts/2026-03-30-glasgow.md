Gave an invited talk at the University of Glasgow as part of the Network on Mathematical Data Science for Materials Science workshop (Workshop on the Interface of Mathematics and Machine Learning for Applications in Materials Science). The talk, *Data-driven compound identification with atmospheric mass spectrometry*, covered our recent work on machine learning approaches for identifying atmospheric compounds using mass spectrometry data.
