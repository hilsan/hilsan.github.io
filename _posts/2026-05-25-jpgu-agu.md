Spent a week in Chiba, Japan for the [JpGU-AGU Joint Meeting 2026](https://www.jpgu.org/meeting_e2026/). It was a really well organized event, and great to see the breadth of atmospheric chemistry research happening in Japan. I gave a contributed talk, *Towards atmospheric compound identification using simulated electron ionization mass spectra*, and also served as a poster judge for the meeting.

After the conference, I joined a two day excursion to the volcanoes Hakone and Fuji, organized as part of the meeting and led by Prof. Kazutaka Mannen of the Hot Springs Research Institute of Kanagawa Prefecture.
