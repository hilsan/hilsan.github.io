Gave a contributed talk at the [Chemical Compounds Space Conference (CCSC 2026)](https://ccsc2026.github.io/) in Munich, Germany. The talk, *Towards atmospheric compound identification using simulated electron ionization mass spectra*, presented our work on using simulated EI mass spectra for improved identification of atmospheric compounds.
